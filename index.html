Hello!


Allow me to introduce myself.

I am a third-year Ph.D student in chemical and biomolecular engineering at the University of Notre Dame. I am a budding scientist, active musician, and passionate vegan.


I am advised by Dr. Edward J. Maginn, whose group performs atomistic molecular dynamics and monte carlo simulations to predict thermodynamic and transport properties of materials. In particular, I am part of the Materials Science of Actinides project, where I use computer simulation to better understand the behavior of actinyl ions in aqueous solution.


I enjoy studying thermodynamics, slapping the bass, and eating falafel.
