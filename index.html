Hello!


I am a fourth-year Ph.D candidate in chemical and biomolecular engineering at the University of Notre Dame. I am a budding scientist, active musician, and passionate vegan.


As a member of the Maginn group, I use classical molecular dynamics to calculate thermodynamic and transport properties of aqueous uranyl ions, which are primary components of nuclear waste. I'm also trying to understand the aggregation behavior of a new class of materials, uranyl nanoclusters.


In my spare time, I enjoy studying statistical mechanics, slapping the bass, and eating falafel.
